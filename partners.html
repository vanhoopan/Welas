Our Trusted Partners

Collaborating for equitable healthcare access

Shared Values, Shared Mission

Welas partners with organizations that believe in using technology to break down healthcare access barriers. Our collaborators include rural hospitals, nonprofit clinics, global health NGOs, and academic institutions.

Types of Partnerships

  • Hospital Collaborations: Co-developing AI tools and platforms that integrate directly with clinical workflows.
  • Nonprofit Clinics: Providing web and data infrastructure to enhance outreach, patient care, and reporting.
  • Health NGOs: Joint programs and grant proposals for expanding technological equity in underserved regions.
  • Academic Institutions: Mentorship, research, and capstone collaborations to advance impact-driven innovation.
